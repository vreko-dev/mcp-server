The Pioneer Program rewards early adopters who help shape SnapBack's future.

## FAQ

### When does the program end?

The Pioneer Program ends when SnapBack officially launches.
After launch, benefits are locked in but new points can't be earned.

### What happens to my benefits after launch?

All earned benefits are permanent:
- Guardians keep lifetime Pro forever
- Cultivators get 1 year free, then 50% off
- Everyone keeps their earned discounts

### Can I lose my tier?

No. Once you reach a tier, you keep it. Points never expire.

### Does joining mean I share my code?

No. Pioneer participation is separate from telemetry.
You can be a Pioneer without sharing any data beyond your account info.
