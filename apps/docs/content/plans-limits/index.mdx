## Tier Details

### Free {#free}

Perfect for individual developers trying SnapBack.

**Key Features:**
- Unlimited local snapshots (you control retention)
- Local MCP scan only — no cloud scoring
- Watch, Warn, Block protection levels
- Community support

**Limitations:**
- No cloud backup
- No cloud-based Guardian AI detection
- No team collaboration features

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll have immediate access to new features. When downgrading, your data remains safe:

- **Local snapshots**: Always preserved (under your control)
- **Cloud backups**: Retained for 30 days after downgrade, then deleted
- **Team data**: Exported before team plan cancellation

### What happens to my data if I cancel?

- **Free tier**: Local snapshots remain on your machine indefinitely
- **Paid tiers**: Cloud backups are retained for 30 days, then permanently deleted
- **Export**: Download all your data before cancellation via the dashboard

### Do you offer non-profit or educational discounts?

Yes! We offer 50% discounts for verified non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Contact sales@snapback.dev with proof of status.

### How does the free trial work?

Solo and Team plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. Full access to all features during the trial period.

### Can I use SnapBack offline?

Yes! The core extension and local snapshots work completely offline. Cloud features (Guardian AI detection, cloud backup, team sharing) require internet connectivity.

## Need Help Choosing?

- **Solo**: Individual professional, need cloud backup + AI detection
- **Team**: 2-10 developers, need shared policies and team collaboration
- **Enterprise**: Organization-wide deployment, compliance requirements, SSO/SAML
